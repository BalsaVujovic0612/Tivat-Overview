Our Location

Description: Tivat is a charming coastal town located in the
Bay of Kotor in Montenegro, known for its beautiful scenery
and growing popularity in luxury nautical tourism.
In recent years, Tivat has gained international
attention thanks to Porto Montenegro, a world-class marina that has become
a popular destination for yacht owners from around the globe.
Besides Porto Montenegro, visitors to Tivat can enjoy stunning beaches,
cultural sites, and scenic mountain views that make it a unique spot in the Adriatic.
